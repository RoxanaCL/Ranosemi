Problem Statement & Goal

Traditional banking and finance apps lack a cohesive and intuitive solution to manage various aspects of users' financial lives. Users often struggle to access a consolidated view of their financial health, investments, and educational resources. Additionally, the absence of personalized insights and ethical banking options further hinders users' ability to align their financial decisions with their values.

The goal of this project is to design and develop a Holistic Financial Dashboard that seamlessly integrates users' accounts, investments, budgeting, educational content, community insights, and ethical banking opportunities.

Addressing these pain points, FinConnect aims to empower users with the tools and knowledge they need to make informed financial decisions, improve their financial literacy, and contribute to a sustainable future. The dashboard will strive to create a unified, engaging, and personalized experience that fosters a sense of financial well-being and community among users. Through a user-first approach, the goal is to provide a transformative solution that goes beyond traditional banking apps, catering to users' comprehensive financial needs and aspirations.
