Project Background

About the Project

The client is an American transportation firm representative. I worked on this project with 16 other designers from Canada and India. Our main goal was to make the process as simple as possible for both logisticians and carriers. Throughout this project, I stayed in touch with the Product Owners, Developers, Business Analysts, and Designers.

The goal of the Project

Finding the best approach to meet client needs and those of web application users is the project's aim. This continuing process's framework was developed using applied UX techniques, empathy, curiosity, and communication.

Principal responsibilities

Principal duties include applying the design system, removing inconsistencies, building libraries for components, and consulting with clients about new and current templates and how they might assist unify the final product. Daily international team meetings, the discovery and application of best practices, and developer talks about UI improvement based on UX research and discovery.
